Lasagna

A hot dish made with pasta meats and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Feta Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatos
  • Tomato paste/sauce
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the spices and meat with the tomato sauce in seperate bowl
  2. Layer the Lasagna noodles in the pan and the set the oven to pre-heat 400f
  3. Alternate the meat sauce and layers of Lasagna noodles, building them from the bottom up
  4. Place the pan in the oven after the oven has reached the 400f
  5. Wait apox. 50 min or untill the top is crunchy and golden brown.
